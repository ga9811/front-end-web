Services

At Friendly Paws Pet Grooming we offer a wide variety of services to fit the needs of pet owners and pets alike. Drop-off service is available, but we also encourage you to stay in our comfortable waiting area complete with complimentary refreshments.

  • Bath and Brush: We offer scented, unscented and medicated shampoos and conditioners. Odor-removing formulas are available for special circumstances such as skunk encounters, etc.
  • Comfort-Clip: Guaranteed to provide your pet with a coat at a comfortable length that is both stylish and low-maintenance. Best for active pets or those with busy owners.
  • Runway Cut: For those owners who prefer a breed-specific cut that would wow the judges at Westminster. Choose from our catalog of professional grooming styles.
  • Purrfect Fluff: Even the self-cleaning cat can sometimes use a little TLC. We guarantee a trauma-free cleaning and de-matting for even the most cantankerous kitty.
  • Full Froo-Froo: Full service bath and brush, clip of choice, painted nails, ribbons or neckerchief, and optional perfume. A spa treatment for your best friend.
